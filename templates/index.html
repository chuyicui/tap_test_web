UPDRS Finger Tapping Test

Professional Assessment Tool

What is this test?

The UPDRS (Unified Parkinson's Disease Rating Scale) finger tapping test is a standardized assessment that measures fine motor control and coordination. This digital version provides precise measurements and detailed analysis of your finger movement patterns.

Test Process:

1. Mood Assessment

Rate how you're feeling today

2. Hand Testing

Test both hands sequentially

3. Results & Report

Download detailed analysis

Requirements:

  • Camera access for hand tracking
  • Clear view of your hands
  • About 3-5 minutes total time
